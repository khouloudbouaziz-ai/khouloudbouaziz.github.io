01

Smart QA — Intelligent Test Automation

2025 LLM · Full-Stack

At Freedom Of Dev, I faced a real challenge: QA teams spending hours writing test cases manually. I built a platform where an LLM reads Azure DevOps user stories and writes the tests itself. Playwright runs them live, Entra ID handles auth, and dashboards give teams instant visibility. The result — dramatically less manual work, more coverage, fewer surprises in production.

02

Real-Time Anomaly Detection in Water Flow

2024 ML · IoT

Water infrastructure failures are costly — and often silent until it's too late. At the Digital Research Center of Sfax, I built a system that detects leaks and pressure anomalies the moment they happen, using an ensemble of Isolation Forest, LSTM, and LOF on live IoT streams. Early detection, before operators even notice anything is wrong.

PythonLSTMIsolation Forest LOFIoTTime Series

03

Ethical Bias Evaluation & Mitigation in ML

2024 XAI · Ethics

A model that's accurate but unfair isn't good enough. This project built a full fairness audit pipeline — measuring bias with AIF360, explaining decisions with LIME and SHAP, and offering mitigation strategies through an interactive Streamlit interface. The goal: make ethical AI accessible, not just theoretical.

AIF360SHAPLIME scikit-learnStreamlitXAI

04

NLP Emotion Classification System

2024 NLP · Deep Learning

Text carries emotion — and machines can learn to hear it. This end-to-end NLP pipeline takes raw text, runs it through NLTK preprocessing, TF-IDF feature extraction, and an LSTM classifier, then serves predictions live via Streamlit. Built to understand language as humans write it.

Solar Panel Robot Trajectory Optimizer
